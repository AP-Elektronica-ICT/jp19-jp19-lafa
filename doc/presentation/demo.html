Analysis

Sensoren

PH

  • Type: DFRobot-SEN-0161
  • Analog Voltage: 0-5V
  • Attiny -> ADC to I²C

* Has to be in a liquid at all times

Humidity and Air temperature

  • Type: HIH6030
  • Communication: I²C
  • Address: 0x27
  • Temperature range: -40°C to 100°C

Water Level

  • Analog Voltage: 0-5V
  • Attiny -> ADC to I²C
  • Measurement range: 40 mm

* Doesn't dry fast enough.

Water Temperature

  • Type: DS18B20+
  • Communication: One-Wire
  • Temperature range: -55°C to 125°C
  • Accuracy: ± 0.5 °C

Light

  • Resistance: 5KΩ - 15KΩ
  • Voltage divider -> attiny -> I²C

* Inaccurate due to reference voltage

Actuators

Light

  • Power supply: 12V @ 1A/m
  • LED Ratio 1 blue/ 3 red

Flow Pump

  • Type: BARWIG0444
  • Power rating: 6-12V @ 1.2A
  • Fitting: 10mm
  • Flowrate: 600 l/h

Food Pump

  • Type: INTLLAB Peristaltic pump
  • Power rating: 6-12V @ 400mA
  • Fitting: 3mm
  • Flowrate: 100 ml/min
